Book 1 of The Cosmic Chronicles

Starfall Echoes

Published October 2021
Genre Sci-Fantasy / Space Opera
Pages 480

Synopsis

When an ancient stellar prophecy awakens, a disgraced astromancer must team up with a rogue elementalist to stop the collision of two planetary systems.

Elara Vance was once the brightest star at the Aethelgard Academy, until her untested theories on void magic got her exiled. Now, working as a ship mechanic on the rusted asteroid belt of Cygnus-9, she believes her days of wielding cosmic elements are over.

But when a falling star crashes near her settlement carrying a metallic cipher and a dying soldier, Elara is thrust into a galactic conspiracy older than the known universe.
