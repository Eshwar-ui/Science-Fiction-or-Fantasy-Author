The Architecture of Loss

Elara Vance was once the brightest star at the Aethelgard Academy, until her untested theories on void magic got her exiled. Now, working as a ship mechanic on the rusted asteroid belt of Cygnus-9, she believes her days of wielding cosmic elements are over.

But when a falling star crashes near her settlement carrying a metallic cipher and a dying soldier, Elara is thrust into a galactic conspiracy older than the known universe. To decipher the code and save the systems, she must venture into the Starfall—a region of space where physical laws are rewritten by the heartbeat of a dying god.

Alongside Kaelen, a rogue elementalist with shadows in his past, Elara must master the forbidden voids she once feared, or watch the galaxy burn in the fires of a new dawn.
