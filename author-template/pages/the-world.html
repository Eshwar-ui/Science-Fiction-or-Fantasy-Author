The Six Kingdoms (Planets)

Caelum

The capital planet. A gas giant with floating cities tethered by anti-gravity anchors. The seat of the Sovereign.

Ignis Prime

A volcanic wasteland rich in starmetal. The industrial heart of the system, inhabited by Pyromancers.

Aquilon

An ocean world where bioluminescent marine life dictates the flow of elemental water spells.

Stellar Magic (Astromancy)

Drawing from the Void

Astromancy is the practice of drawing energy from celestial bodies. Astromancers must physically align themselves with a dying star or a nascent nebula to channel raw cosmic power.

  • Solar Weaving: Manipulating light and heat. Highly destructive.
  • Void Walking: Bending space using dark matter. Allows for short-range teleportation.
  • Gravokinesis: The rarest ability, restricted to the Royal Bloodline, controlling gravity fields.
