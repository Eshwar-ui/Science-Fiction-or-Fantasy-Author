About The Author

Astrophysicist Turned World-Builder

The Journey to The Stars

Aria Vance never intended to be a writer. Her early career was spent with her eyes glued to telescope arrays, analyzing stellar frequencies and mapping distant exoplanets as an astrophysicist. However, the vast silence of space left her imagination craving something more—a narrative to fill the cosmic void.

She began writing Starfall Echoes in the margins of her research notes. What started as an exercise in creative escapism evolved into the sprawling Cosmic Chronicles universe.

Today, Aria lives in the Pacific Northwest with her two cats, Nebula and Quasar, and an ever-growing collection of medieval weaponry and vintage star charts.
